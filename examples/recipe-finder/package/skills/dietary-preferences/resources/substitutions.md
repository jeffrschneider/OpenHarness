# Ingredient Substitutions

Common substitutions organized by what you're replacing.

## Dairy Substitutions

| Replace | With | Notes |
|---------|------|-------|
| Milk | Oat milk, almond milk, coconut milk | Oat is creamiest for cooking |
| Heavy cream | Coconut cream, cashew cream | Coconut adds slight flavor |
| Butter | Vegan butter, coconut oil | Coconut oil for baking, vegan butter for savory |
| Cheese | Nutritional yeast, cashew cheese | Nutritional yeast for "cheesy" flavor |
| Yogurt | Coconut yogurt, oat yogurt | Check for added sugars |
| Sour cream | Cashew cream + lemon, coconut cream | Add acid for tang |

## Egg Substitutions

| Replace | With | Best For |
|---------|------|----------|
| 1 egg | 1 tbsp ground flax + 3 tbsp water | Baking (binding) |
| 1 egg | 1/4 cup applesauce | Baking (moisture) |
| 1 egg | 1/2 mashed banana | Baking (sweetness OK) |
| 1 egg | 3 tbsp aquafaba | Meringues, light batters |
| 1 egg | Commercial egg replacer | General purpose |

## Gluten Substitutions

| Replace | With | Notes |
|---------|------|-------|
| All-purpose flour | 1:1 GF flour blend | Results vary by brand |
| Soy sauce | Tamari, coconut aminos | Tamari is richer |
| Breadcrumbs | GF breadcrumbs, crushed GF crackers, almond flour | Almond flour won't crisp as well |
| Pasta | Rice pasta, chickpea pasta, zucchini noodles | Chickpea has more protein |
| Flour tortillas | Corn tortillas, lettuce wraps | Check corn tortillas are pure corn |

## Meat Substitutions (for vegetarian/vegan)

| Replace | With | Best For |
|---------|------|----------|
| Ground beef | Lentils, crumbled tempeh, TVP | Tacos, pasta sauce |
| Chicken | Tofu, seitan, jackfruit | Depends on texture needed |
| Bacon | Tempeh bacon, coconut bacon, mushrooms | Mushrooms for umami |
| Fish sauce | Soy sauce + seaweed | Add small piece of kombu |

## Tips for Substitutions

1. **Start with less** - You can always add more
2. **Consider the role** - Is it for flavor, texture, binding, or leavening?
3. **Test first** - Try substitutions in small batches
4. **Combine when needed** - Sometimes two subs work better than one
5. **Adjust other ingredients** - Subs may affect salt, sweetness, or moisture
