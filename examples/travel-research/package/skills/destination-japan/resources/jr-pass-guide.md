# Japan Rail Pass Guide

## Overview

The Japan Rail (JR) Pass is a cost-effective rail pass for tourists visiting Japan. It offers unlimited travel on most JR trains, including most Shinkansen (bullet trains).

## Pass Types & Prices (2024)

## What's Included

### Included
- Most JR trains nationwide
- Shinkansen (except Nozomi and Mizuho)
- JR local and rapid trains
- JR buses (some routes)
- JR Ferry to Miyajima
- Tokyo Monorail (to Haneda Airport)

### Not Included
- Nozomi and Mizuho Shinkansen (fastest trains)
- Non-JR private railways
- Subways (except JR lines)
- Most buses

## Is the JR Pass Worth It?

### Good Value If:
- Tokyo + Kyoto + other cities
- 3+ long-distance trips in 7 days
- Exploring multiple regions

### Example: Tokyo-Kyoto-Osaka Route
- Tokyo to Kyoto: ¥14,170 one way
- Kyoto to Osaka: ¥580 one way
- Round trip alone: ¥29,500

### Not Worth It If:
- Staying in one city
- Only 1-2 short trips
- Using mostly buses/private lines

## How to Use

### Purchasing
1. Buy exchange order before arriving in Japan
2. Can now purchase in Japan at major stations
3. Online purchase available at japanrailpass.net

### Activation
1. Go to JR ticket office (Midori-no-madoguchi)
2. Present passport and exchange order
3. Choose activation date (within 30 days)
4. Receive actual pass

### Using the Pass
- Show pass at manned gate (not automatic gates)
- Reserve seats at ticket office (free with pass)
- Unreserved cars: Just board and sit

## Seat Reservations

### Free With Pass
- Shinkansen reserved seats
- Limited Express reserved seats

### When to Reserve
- Peak seasons (Golden Week, Obon, New Year)
- Popular routes during rush hours
- If traveling with children (guaranteed seats)

### How to Reserve
- JR ticket office (any major station)
- JR ticket machines (some locations)
- Online (limited availability)

## Regional Passes

If not traveling extensively, consider regional passes:

| Pass | Coverage | 7-day Price |
|------|----------|-------------|
| JR East Pass (Tohoku) | Tokyo + Northern Japan | ¥30,000 |
| JR West Pass (Sanyo) | Osaka-Hiroshima-Fukuoka | ¥25,000 |
| JR Kyushu Pass | Kyushu Island | ¥20,000 |

## Tips

1. **Calculate first**: Add up planned journeys at hyperdia.com
2. **Activate strategically**: Start pass on first long-distance day
3. **Use it fully**: Take day trips to maximize value
4. **Reserve popular routes**: Golden route (Tokyo-Kyoto) fills up
5. **Keep it safe**: Replacement is difficult
