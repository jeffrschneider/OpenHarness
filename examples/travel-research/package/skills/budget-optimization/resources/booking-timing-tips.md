# When to Book: Timing Guide

Optimal booking windows for different travel components.

## Flights

### Domestic Flights
| Timing | Recommendation |
|--------|----------------|
| Sweet spot | 1-3 months before departure |
| Too early | >4 months (prices often drop) |
| Too late | <2 weeks (prices spike) |
| Best day to book | Tuesday-Wednesday |
| Best day to fly | Tuesday, Wednesday, Saturday |

### International Flights
| Timing | Recommendation |
|--------|----------------|
| Sweet spot | 2-8 months before departure |
| Peak season | Book 4-6 months out |
| Off-peak | 6-8 weeks can work |
| Best months | January, September (fewer travelers booking) |

### Flight Tips
- Set price alerts on Google Flights, Hopper
- Check both one-way combinations and round trips
- Nearby airports can save significantly
- Error fares: Follow deal sites for mistakes

## Hotels

### Standard Hotels
| Timing | Recommendation |
|--------|----------------|
| Sweet spot | 2-3 weeks before (flexible) |
| Book early | For peak dates, special events |
| Last minute | Can score deals if flexible |
| Best rates | Direct booking or loyalty programs |

### Unique Accommodations
| Type | Book Ahead |
|------|------------|
| Popular Airbnbs | 2-3 months |
| Ryokans (Japan) | 1-3 months |
| Safari lodges | 6-12 months |
| Overwater bungalows | 6+ months |
| Christmas markets | 6+ months |

### Hotel Tips
- Book refundable, then check for better rates later
- Call hotel directly for best rate guarantee
- Check rates on multiple platforms
- Points/miles often best value for luxury

## Activities & Tours

### Book Far Ahead (3-6 months)
- Machu Picchu permits
- Uffizi Gallery (Florence)
- Anne Frank House (Amsterdam)
- Ghibli Museum (Japan)
- Popular cooking classes
- Michelin-starred restaurants

### Book 2-4 Weeks Ahead
- Walking tours
- Day trips
- Most museum tickets
- Guided tours
- Restaurant reservations (non-peak)

### Day-Of Usually Fine
- Most museums (except major ones)
- Public transportation
- Casual restaurants
- Self-guided activities

### Activity Tips
- Check if timed entry is required
- Book directly when possible (skip reseller fees)
- Read cancellation policies
- Have backup activities ready

## Transportation

### Trains
| Region | Booking |
|--------|---------|
| Europe (high-speed) | 1-3 months for best prices |
| Japan Rail Pass | Before arrival |
| Amtrak | 1-2 months |
| Eurail Pass | Anytime before travel |

### Rental Cars
| Timing | Recommendation |
|--------|----------------|
| Sweet spot | 2-6 weeks ahead |
| Peak season | Book early, then rebook if prices drop |
| Best rates | Compare aggregators + direct |
| Airport vs. city | City pickups often cheaper |

## Seasonal Considerations

### Peak Season (Book Early)
- Everything books up fast
- Prices don't drop last-minute
- Limited availability for popular items
- Book 3-6 months ahead

### Shoulder Season (Moderate Lead Time)
- Good availability
- Prices relatively stable
- 1-3 months usually fine
- Can find last-minute deals

### Off-Peak (More Flexibility)
- Often cheaper closer to date
- Hotels want to fill rooms
- 2-4 weeks often works
- Watch for closures/reduced hours
