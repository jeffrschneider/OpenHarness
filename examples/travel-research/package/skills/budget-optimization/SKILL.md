# Budget Optimization

## Overview

This skill provides strategies for getting maximum value from a travel budget, including optimal booking timing, shoulder season benefits, and smart tradeoffs.

## Usage

The main agent and all researcher sub-agents reference this skill when:
- Comparing options within budget constraints
- Suggesting cost-saving alternatives
- Timing bookings for best prices
- Prioritizing spending across categories

## Key Strategies

### Timing
- Flights: Book 6-8 weeks ahead for domestic, 2-3 months for international
- Hotels: Often cheaper closer to date (except peak seasons)
- Activities: Early booking discounts for popular attractions

### Shoulder Seasons
- Just before/after peak season
- Better prices, fewer crowds
- Weather usually still good

### Smart Tradeoffs
- Location vs. amenities: Central location often worth premium
- Breakfast included: Can offset daily food budget
- Refundable vs. non-refundable: Risk vs. savings

### Hidden Costs to Watch
- Resort fees
- Baggage fees on budget airlines
- Tourist taxes
- Currency conversion fees
